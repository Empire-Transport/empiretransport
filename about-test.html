    About Sims Empire Transport


    Driving Excellence in Transportation for Over 9 Years.


    Sims Empire Transport is a proudly Black-owned transportation service with a strong track record of over nine years in operation. We are committed to providing comprehensive fleet and transportation solutions, initially serving the White River and Mbombela areas with dedication. In 2024, we expanded our reach to include the Johannesburg metro, further demonstrating our growth and commitment to serving a wider clientele.


    At the heart of our success is our exceptional team. We are a collective of highly trained and skilled professionals driven by a singular goal: to deliver the finest customer service. Through their consistent excellence, we have built a company renowned for its reliability, advanced transportation services, and unwavering commitment to our clients.


    Our journey has been supported by key stakeholders within the transportation industry, coupled with the invaluable expertise of Mr. Ndlovu and Mr. Mnisi. Their leadership has been instrumental in steering Sims Empire Transport to the success we enjoy today. We believe in continuous improvement, taking the time necessary to meticulously review our transport services and fleet management practices to consistently elevate our service standards.


    We offer a diverse range of transportation services tailored to meet the needs of individuals, groups, and corporate clients, including:


    • Private Hire: Reliable and comfortable transportation for various occasions.
    • Tours: Efficient transportation for group tours.
    • Scholar Transportation: Safe and dependable transport solutions for students.

    At Sims Empire Transport, we are more than just a transportation service; we are a partner dedicated to providing exceptional experiences and exceeding your expectations. We look forward to serving your transportation needs.
